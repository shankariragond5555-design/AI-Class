01.Difference between / and // in Python:
Ans:-
/ → Division operator (returns float result)
// → Floor division operator (returns quotient without decimal part)

Example:

10 / 3   # 3.3333
10 // 3  # 3


02.What is the Modulus operator (%) is used for:
Ans:-
Finding the remainder after division.

Example:

10 % 3   # 1


03.What is the Exponentiation operator in Python:
Ans:-
** is used for power/exponent.

Example:
